about me

I’m a UX/UI designer and developer living in Chicago, IL. I’m passionate about the user’s experience and making sure that the product is inclusive and equitable. Whether that’s by testing across devices to ensure compatibility, or providing accessibility options so the next billion users are able to enjoy the product.
